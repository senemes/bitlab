The Boeing 777, commonly referred to as the Triple Seven

The Boeing 777, commonly referred to as the Triple Seven, is an American long-range wide-body airliner developed and manufactured by Boeing Commercial Airplanes. It is the world's largest twinjet.The 777 was designed to bridge the gap between Boeing's other wide body airplanes, the twin-engined 767 and quad-engined 747, and to replace older DC-10s and L-1011 trijets.

Three reasons Biden's Covid bill is a big deal

TLast year, the US Congress passed approximately $3tn in Covid-related aid. Biden's legislation, aimed primarily at the economic impact of the pandemic, adds another $1.9tn to the total. It dwarfs the $831bn American Recovery and Reinvestment Act, passed to address the Great Recession in the early days of the Obama administration (thanks in no small part to then Vice-President Biden's lobbying efforts). It is well over half of the $3.5tn that the US government brought in as revenue in 2019.
